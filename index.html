David Oduor | WDD 130

Hello! My name is David Oduor, and I come from Muhoroni in Kisumu, Kenya. I am currently a student in web development, a course that fuels my passion to grow socially, academically, and professionally. Growing up and working in the informal settlements of Mathare, I have witnessed firsthand the challenges many face after completing Form 4—limited opportunities, poverty, and a lack of hope. These experiences have inspired me to be a catalyst for positive change. Through the skills and knowledge I am gaining, I aspire to empower my community, uplift those around me, and create opportunities for others to succeed. My journey is driven by a deep belief that education, faith, and service can transform lives, and I am committed to being a part of that transformation by helping others discover their potential and achieve their dreams.

Kenya
