My Favourite City

Zanzibar Stone Town in Tanzania is my favorite city because of its rich history, beautiful culture, and unique architecture. The city is known for its narrow winding streets, historic buildings, and vibrant markets that reflect a blend of African, Arab, and European influences. Walking through Stone Town feels like stepping back in time, with carved wooden doors, old mosques, and bustling local shops everywhere. I also love the warm atmosphere of the people and the amazing view of the Indian Ocean. The mix of history, culture, and natural beauty makes Zanzibar Stone Town a very special and unforgettable place for me
